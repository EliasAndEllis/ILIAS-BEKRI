ILSY (International Language School for Youth)

  • Duration: 6, 9, or 11 months
  • Levels: Beginner to Advanced
  • Lesson Structure: Includes core language, project-based learning, and youth-focused activities
  • Target Audience: Designed for students under 18 (the "Y" stands for Youth)
  • Includes progress assessments and graduation certificates
  • More cost-effective for long-term students
  • Visa support provided for longer durations

Academic Year Program (AY)

The Academic Year Program is designed for students seeking an immersive, long-term language learning experience with a focus on academic and professional development.

Program Details

  • Duration: 6, 9, or 11 months
  • Levels: Intermediate to Advanced
  • Lesson Structure: Combines intensive language training with academic skills workshops and career-oriented electives
  • Start Dates: Fixed intakes in January, April, June, and September

Key Benefits

  • Preparation for official language certifications (e.g., TOEFL, IELTS, Cambridge)
  • Internship opportunities in select destinations
  • Personalized academic advising and progress tracking
  • Graduation certificate recognized by partner institutions

University Preparation (UP)

The University Preparation program equips international students with the language, academic, and cultural skills needed to succeed in English-speaking higher education institutions.

Program Details

  • Duration: 6, 9, or 12 months
  • Levels: Upper-Intermediate to Advanced
  • Lesson Structure: Focuses on academic English, university application processes, and study skills
  • Start Dates: Fixed intakes in January, April, and September

Key Benefits

  • Guaranteed admission to partner universities upon successful completion
  • Training for standardized tests (e.g., TOEFL, IELTS, SAT)
  • Guidance on personal statements and university applications
  • Workshops on academic writing, critical thinking, and presentation skills

Academic Programs and Pathways (APP)

The Academic Programs and Pathways is tailored for students aiming to bridge the gap between their current academic level and the requirements of top-tier universities or professional programs.

Program Details

  • Age: 16Y+ or High schools, Universities, Professionals
  • Lessons: 32 lessons (21 hours) or 26 lessons (17 hours)
  • Levels: Beginner to Intermediate to Advanced
  • Start Dates: September, November, January, April, June
  • Length Options:
    • 6 months or Language Semester (LYS)
    • 9 months or Language Year (LY)
    • 11 months or Language Year Extended (LYX)

Key Benefits

  • Personalized pathway plans based on student goals and proficiency
  • Support for university applications and visa processes
  • Access to EFâ€™s global network of partner institutions
  • Progress monitoring with regular feedback and assessments

Common Information Across All EF Courses

Course Start Dates

Most EF courses start every Monday, year-round. Some specialty programs have fixed start dates.

Accommodation Options

  • Host Family: The most popular option for full cultural immersion.
  • EF Campus: Available in select cities, offering a social and convenient living experience.
  • EF Residence: Ideal for students who want more independence.

Activities

EF offers a mix of free and paid activities such as excursions, social events, cultural outings, and local trips. These enhance the student experience outside the classroom.

Types of Schools

  • Permanent EF Schools: Open year-round, located in major cities globally.
  • EF Summer Schools: Seasonal locations catering to short-term students, typically younger learners.
